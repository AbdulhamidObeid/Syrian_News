# Facebook & Instagram Playbook

## 1. Algorithm & Frequency
*   **Facebook Core Focus:** Community building, discussion-starters, and highly shareable local news.
*   **Frequency:** 3-5 posts per day. Spread them out (Morning, Noon, Evening) to hit different audience segments.
*   **Instagram Core Focus:** Visual identity, typography, and deep-dive carousels.
*   **Frequency:** 1 Reel daily, 1 Carousel every 2 days.

## 2. Tone of Voice
*   **Facebook:** Interactive and community-driven. Ask questions at the end of captions to drive comments (e.g., "What do you think about this decision?").
*   **Instagram:** Aesthetic and slightly more formal. Let the imagery speak. Keep captions shorter and utilize appropriate, targeted hashtags.

## 3. Formatting
*   Always use the `portrait_1080x1350` aspect ratio. Facebook and Instagram algorithms favor vertical real estate in the feed.
*   Never use links in Instagram captions (they are unclickable). Use "Link in bio" if a CTA is required.
