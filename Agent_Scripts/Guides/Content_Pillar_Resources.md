## 💡 هل تعلم؟ — Did You Know (Daily · 12:00)

**Template:** `pillar_didyouknow.html`  
**Frequency:** Daily  
**Goal:** Educate & spark curiosity. Drive saves and shares.

### Where to Get Content

| Source | Type | Link |
|--------|------|------|
| Wikipedia Arabic | Syria history, geography, culture | `ar.wikipedia.org/wiki/سوريا` |
| وزارة السياحة السورية | Historical sites, landmarks | `ministry.tourism.gov.sy` |
| موسوعة الشعر العربي | Syrian literature & poets | `adab.com` |
| تاريخ سوريا (Archive.org) | Historical documents | `archive.org` |
| BBC Arabic Syria | Cultural facts in the news | `bbc.com/arabic` |

### Content Rules
- **Format:** One surprising fact per post. Short (3–5 lines max).
- **Tone:** Curious, warm, educational — never dry.
- **Topics to rotate:** History → Geography → Culture → Food → Famous Syrians → Natural landmarks
- **Opening words:** Use "هل تعلم أن..." or "اكتشف أن..." to hook the reader.
- **Hashtags:** `#هل_تعلم #سوريا #HashSYR24 #تاريخ_سوريا`

### Example Topics
- قلعة حلب هي من أقدم وأضخم القلاع في العالم
- نهر الفرات يعبر سوريا لمسافة 680 كيلومتراً
- مدينة دمشق تُعدّ من أقدم المدن المأهولة في التاريخ
- الكمأة السورية تُصدَّر إلى أكثر من 30 دولة حول العالم

## 📊 رأيك يهمنا — Poll / Opinion (Every 2 Days · 20:00)

**Template:** `pillar_poll.html`  
**Frequency:** Every 2 days  
**Goal:** Maximum engagement — comments, reactions, shares.

### Where to Get Poll Ideas

| Source | How to Use |
|--------|-----------|
| Facebook Comments on recent posts | Mine recurring questions/complaints |
| Syrian news headlines (سانا / عنب بلدي) | Turn breaking news into a yes/no opinion |
| Telegram group discussions | What are Syrians debating right now? |
| Google Trends Arabic Syria | Find trending search topics |
| Latest economic stats (gold, fuel prices) | "هل تتوقع أن تنخفض الأسعار؟" |

### Content Rules
- **Question structure:** Open-ended or binary choice works best.
- **2–4 answer options** shown on the graphic.
- **Topics to avoid:** Religion, sectarianism, direct political criticism of individuals.
- **Topics that perform best:** Economy, services, sports predictions, daily life in Syria.
- **Tone:** Neutral — present both sides without bias.

### High-Performing Poll Formats
- "ما أكبر تحدٍّ تواجهه يومياً في سوريا؟ 🇸🇾"
- "هل تعتقد أن أسعار الكهرباء ستنخفض قبل نهاية العام؟"
- "من سيفوز بكأس العالم 2026؟ صوّت الآن! ⚽"
- "هل تدعم مشاريع إعادة إعمار دمشق؟"

## 📈 إنفوغرافيك — Infographic (Every 3 Days · 15:00)

**Template:** `pillar_infographic.html`  
**Frequency:** Every 3 days  
**Goal:** Authority-building. Heavy shares, saves, and media pickup.

### Where to Get Data

| Source | Data Type | Link |
|--------|-----------|------|
| البنك الدولي — Syria | GDP, poverty, trade data | `data.worldbank.org/country/SY` |
| UNOCHA Syria | Humanitarian stats, displacement | `reliefweb.int/country/syr` |
| المكتب المركزي للإحصاء السوري | Official Syrian statistics | `cbssyr.sy` |
| مؤشر مدركات الفساد | Transparency rankings | `transparency.org/en/countries/SyAR` |
| Trading Economics Syria | Inflation, exchange rates | `tradingeconomics.com/Syria` |
| FAO Syria | Agriculture, food security | `fao.org/syria` |
| UNHCR Syria | Refugee return statistics | `unhcr.org/sy` |
| B2B-SY.com | Daily Syrian market prices | `b2b-sy.com` |

### Content Rules
- **Format:** Present 4–6 data points as clear numbered bullets.
- **Always cite the source** in the sub-headline (e.g., "المصدر: البنك الدولي").
- **Use comparison** to give numbers context: "ارتفع بنسبة 40% مقارنة بالعام الماضي".
- **Topics to rotate:** Economy → Reconstruction → Population & Refugees → Agriculture → Energy → Infrastructure
- **Hashtags:** `#إنفوغرافيك #سوريا #اقتصاد_سوريا #HashSYR24`

### Example Topics
- أبرز مؤشرات الاقتصاد السوري 2026
- أعداد العائدين إلى سوريا منذ عام 2024
- إنتاج القمح في سوريا — المقارنة التاريخية
- مستويات الكهرباء في المحافظات السورية

## ⚽ مونديال 2026 — World Cup (Event-Based · During matches)

**Template:** `pillar_worldcup.html`  
**Frequency:** During World Cup 2026 (June–July 2026) — multiple times daily during match days  
**Goal:** Ride the massive World Cup wave. Viral reach and follower growth.

### Where to Get Match Data

| Source | Type | Link |
|--------|------|------|
| FIFA Official | Schedules, results, standings | `fifa.com/worldcup` |
| كووورة | Arabic football scores | `kooora.com` |
| ESPN Arabic | Match reports, stats | `espn.com/arabic` |
| BBC Sport Arabic | Live match coverage | `bbc.com/sport/arabic` |
| Opta Stats | Player performance data | Twitter: `@OptaSTAT` |
| الميدان الرياضي | Arabic sports news | `almaydannet.com` |

### Content Types to Post
1. **Pre-match preview** (2h before kickoff): Teams, prediction
2. **Result post** (within 30min of final whistle): Score, scorers, key moments
3. **Standings update** (after each group stage round): Full table
4. **Star player spotlight**: Top stats after each round
5. **Arab teams focus**: Any match involving Arab nations gets priority

### Content Rules
- Post results **as fast as possible** — speed matters for football news.
- Always mention **Arabic players or Arab nations** in context.
- Use emojis of country flags: 🇸🇾 🇸🇦 🇲🇦 ⚽ 🏆
- **Hashtags:** `#كأس_العالم2026 #مونديال2026 #HashSYR24 #كرة_القدم`
